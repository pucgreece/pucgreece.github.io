Welcome to PUC Greece

Building hope, resilience, and community.

Our Mission

At PUC Greece, we are dedicated to creating safe, supportive, and sustainable environments for communities in need. We work on education, housing, and empowerment projects that foster dignity and long-term opportunity.

Our Impact

From Thessaloniki to refugee camps across Greece, our team has helped build structures, provide educational resources, and bring together volunteers from around the world. Every project represents lives touched and futures transformed.

How to Support

You can support us by volunteering, donating, or partnering with us to extend our reach. Together, we can continue to make a meaningful difference.
