Projects for Underserved Communities: Greece

Empowering communities through design, culture, and compassion.

Our Mission

PUC Greece unites students, engineers, and community leaders to design and construct sustainable infrastructure that supports refugee and local populations in Northern Greece. Our mission is to use engineering as a tool for dignity, education, and opportunity.

Our Impact

Partnering with the YMCA of Thessaloniki, our 2025 team is developing new recreational and community spaces at Camp Drama — providing safe gathering places for families and youth from diverse backgrounds. Each project blends cultural exchange with hands-on learning and design for social good.

How to Support

You can help by donating, partnering, or volunteering to expand our reach. Every contribution strengthens our shared vision of empowerment, inclusion, and hope. Together, we build more than structures — we build community.
