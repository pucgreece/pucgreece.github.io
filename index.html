Projects for Underserved Communities: Greece

Empowering communities through engineering, empathy, and collaboration.

Our Mission

PUC Greece brings students together to co-design sustainable infrastructure with local communities in Greece. Through our partnership with YMCA Thessaloniki, we are creating inclusive spaces that foster resilience, play, and cultural connection.

Our Impact

Our 2025 project at Camp Drama supports refugee and local youth by building a recreational and communal space designed to inspire connection and hope. Each member of our team contributes to this vision through engineering, fundraising, and cultural exchange.

How to Support

You can help by donating, sponsoring materials, or sharing our story. Every contribution fuels the impact of student-driven service projects that transform lives and empower underserved communities.
